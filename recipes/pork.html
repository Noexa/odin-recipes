Korean Pork Stir Fry

Spicy Greatness

Confused on what to make to add some variety to your weeknight meals? Look no further than this recipe for Korean Stir Fried Pork! This pork recipe will make you reach for seconds after a mouthwatering experience. The pork is marinated with Korean chili paste that gives a slight kick that lingers on your tongue. Each bite of pork carries a sweet and spicy punch that smells as delicious as it tastes thanks to the garlic infused chili oil that everything is coated in.

Your house is going to think you're a master chef after they see what you can make with 30 minutes and a few basic ingredients. Did I mention that all the ingredients are cheap to buy? What more can you ask before beside simple, cheap, and delicious weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 onion cut into strips
  • 1 green onion, roughly chopped
  • 1-2 chili peppers
  • 600g pork
  • 2 tbsp Korean chili paste
  • 1 tbsp corn syrup
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp Korean chili flakes
  • 2 tsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp pineapple juice

Steps

  1. Smash the cloves of garlic and finely mince them
  2. Slice the onion into strips
  3. Rough Chop the green onions
  4. Chop the chili pepper small chunks
  5. Cut the pork into bite sized pieces
  6. Mix the other ingredients together and give it a good mix for our marinade
  7. Pour the marinade over the pork and toss together until evenly coated.
  8. Put some oil in a pan and heat it on medium then add the pork
  9. Once the pork is nearly cooked add the vegetables for about a minute
  10. Take everything out and put it onto a serving plate!
  11. Bonus: If you want it to look impressive consider topping it with sesame seeds!
