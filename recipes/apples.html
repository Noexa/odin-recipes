Caramelized Apples

A Sweet Tooth's Dream!

Caramelized apples topped with vanilla ice cream is a classic dessert that is both simple and elegant. The dish starts with fresh apples that are peeled, cored, and sliced into wedges. The apple slices are then saut√©ed in a mixture of butter and brown sugar until they are tender and caramelized. This process brings out the natural sweetness of the apples and creates a rich, buttery sauce that coats each slice. The warm apples are then served on a bed of creamy vanilla ice cream, creating a delightful contrast of hot and cold textures. The combination of flavors and textures is truly divine, making it a perfect ending to any meal.

The dish can be customized to suit personal tastes and preferences. Some people add cinnamon or nutmeg to the apple mixture for a warm, cozy flavor. Others drizzle caramel sauce over the top of the ice cream for an extra indulgent treat. Regardless of how it's prepared, caramelized apples topped with vanilla ice cream is a dessert that is sure to please even the most discerning of palates. It's a classic that never goes out of style, and a perfect way to end any meal on a sweet note.

Ingredients

  • 4 apples
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 pint of vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Pre-eat a skillet to medium heat
  2. While the skillet is heating up, peel the apples and cut them into quarters
  3. Cut each quarter into bite sized slices
  4. Add butter to the skillet and wait until fully melted
  5. Add the apples and cook for about 2 minutes (stir frequently!)
  6. Add the brown sugar and cinnamon and cook for another 6-8 minutes
  7. Turn off the heat and scoop your caramelized apples into a bowl
  8. Take a scoop or two of ice cream and add it on top of the hot apples
  9. Wait 20 seconds to allow the residual heat from the apples to slightly melt the ice cream
  10. Enjoy!
