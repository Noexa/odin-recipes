Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Italian Delight

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a traditional Italian pasta dish that is both simple and flavorful. The dish consists of spaghetti noodles that are cooked until al dente, then tossed with a mixture of garlic, olive oil, red pepper flakes, and parsley. The garlic and olive oil are the stars of the show, providing a rich and savory base that coats each strand of spaghetti. The red pepper flakes add a touch of heat, while the parsley provides a fresh and vibrant contrast. The result is a dish that is both satisfying and refreshing.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a variety of ingredients. Some people add shrimp or chicken to the dish for a protein boost, while others add vegetables such as broccoli or spinach for a more balanced meal. The dish can also be customized to suit individual tastes - some prefer more garlic or red pepper flakes, while others prefer a milder flavor. Regardless of how it's prepared, Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a classic dish that is sure to impress with its simplicity and depth of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add a few tablespoons of salt
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the spaghetti and cook for about 2 minutes less than the package instructions
  3. Heat the olive oil over a medium heat until shimmering
  4. Add the garlic to the olive oil and cook until golden brown (about 2 minutes)
  5. Add the red pepper flakes and cook for 30 more seconds then turn off the heat
  6. Drain the spaghetti and reserve around 1/2 cup of the water
  7. Combine the spaghetti to our garlic infused oil and toss to insure an even coat
  8. Add the parsley and toss again
  9. Salt to taste4
